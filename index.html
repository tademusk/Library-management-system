About Haramya University library

The HU Libraries System is viewed from its strategic importance to the teaching learning research and community services goals of the University. Accordingly, a Library Information System is a keystone of any academic and research exercise.

Obviously, HU Library System has been set up with this certainty. The HU Library system is one of the functional units of the University that is responsible for the provision of Library & Information Services mainly to students, teaching staff & researchers in order to enable them to attain their learning, teaching and research objectives. Haramaya University Libraries are constitutes of the following: Main University Library which is both for male and female and the other two branch libraries: female Library & law Library are situated at the Main Campus.

Main objective of this system

The Library Management System developed for Haramaya University can keep track of data related to library cards, the possible users (i.e. students, teachers, etc) of the library, librarians (i.e.for the main campus library), books and the relationships between them.
